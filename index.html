Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our clinic and home services.

Which areas do you cover for Home Visit services? +
We primarily serve **Pulchowk, Jawalakhel, Sanepa, Jhamsikhel, and Kupandole**. However, for specialized long-term rehabilitation (like Post-Stroke or Post-Surgery), we cover most parts of Kathmandu and Lalitpur.
Do I need a doctor's referral for physiotherapy? +
No, you do not necessarily need a referral. You can book an appointment directly with us. Our therapists will conduct a thorough initial assessment to determine the best course of action.
How long does a typical session last? +
A standard session usually lasts between **45 to 60 minutes**, depending on the complexity of the condition and the type of treatment (manual therapy, exercise rehab, or modalities) required.
What should I prepare for a Home Visit? +
Please ensure there is a relatively quiet space with enough room for a treatment table or exercise mat. Wearing comfortable, loose clothing that allows easy access to the area being treated is highly recommended.
Do you offer discounted treatment packages? +
Yes! We offer discounted **7-day and 15-day packages** for patients requiring daily rehabilitation, such as post-operative recovery or stroke management.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept **Cash, FonePay (QR Scan), and Bank Transfers**. For clinic visits, digital payment via QR is the preferred method for a contactless experience.
